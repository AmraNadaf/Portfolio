Philosophy

"Living is to experience." This is the philosophy I live by. I believe the entirety of our life is about experiencing, building, and becoming the best version of ourselves. Every choice we make, every person we meet, and every challenge we face shapes who we are. For me, life is not about waiting for the perfect moment, but about finding meaning in the imperfect ones. It’s about saying yes to opportunities, learning from mistakes, and embracing the unknown with curiosity instead of fear. I see growth as a constant journey, not a finish line, where each experience adds another layer to who I am becoming. In the end, what matters most is not how much time we had, but how fully we lived it.

I’m someone who’s always chasing new ideas, adventures, and little sparks of inspiration. Whether it’s experimenting with creative projects, diving into a new skill just for the fun of it, or getting lost in a late-night conversation, I like to keep life interesting. I’m curious by nature, drawn to stories—both mine and others’ and I believe even the smallest moments can leave the biggest marks. At my core, I’m just a person trying to create, explore, and leave the world a little better than I found it.
